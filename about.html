Our Story



We started back in 2023 in the hardware and peripherals business with the name of PC Systems. In time we progressed and ‘RigWare.com’ was formed. With the strong communities of architects and gamers who we’ve had the pleasure of knowing as clients and subsequently as friends. RigWare is an online platform where anyone can reach for an exclusive experience and for machines that are perfectly configured. The machines are hand-built with care and attention, immaculate cable management and vigorous testing. Along with machines for Architecture, we have expanded and excelled our expertise in Gaming, Streaming, Video Editing, VFX, Rendering, Stock-Trading PCs and more. These machines are what we know best.
